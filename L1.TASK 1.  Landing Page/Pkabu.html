About _____

The PKaBu Resturant

Welcome to our Family Resturant, we are proud of our freshly prepared food, warm welcome and relaxing atmoshphere. It's the ideal place for those that seek genuine value, whether for a family meal, a relaxing drink or a bit to eat with friends.

The PKaBu Resturant is newly established resturant located in Haldwani. It is owned and operated by an well trained chef with over 20 years of culinary experience.

All our meals are prepared in the traditional uncompromising style, with fresh imported ingredients and cooked to prefection.

We cater for the whole family, so you can have relaxing meal while your kids have fun with our playland bouncy castle and trampoline, cartoon characters like Dora, Oggy and Tom and Jarry and video games like Xbox, Playstation3, or watch movies while sitting on chair. You can also use high speed internet.

Enjoy your meal indoors, on our upstairs patio, relax by our staff and parking is also available.
